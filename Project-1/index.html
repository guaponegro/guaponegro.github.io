Planet Pong

How To Play

1. User will start game by clicking a start game button in the middle of the screen

2. Once you click start, you will have to go through three levels of Pong, each one harder than the last

3. You can move left or right with the arrow keys or with your mouse

4. Win and be champion of the universe! Lose and be disqualified!
